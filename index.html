Mahindra

Off-Road SUV

The Mahindra Thar is a compact, four-wheel drive, off-road SUV manufactured by Indian automaker Mahindra and Mahindra Ltd. It is a lifestyle off-road SUV that combines strong road presence with punchy engines, smooth transmissions, and serious off-road capability. The current-generation model was launched in 2020 and has since managed to sell over 1 lakh units, a testament to the SUV's ever-growing popularity. The Thar is available in several variants, including petrol and diesel options, with a range of transmission choices, including manual and automatic.

In terms of specifications, the Thar boasts a range of engines, including a 2L turbo petrol engine, a 2.2L diesel engine, and a 1.5L diesel engine. The SUV also features a 6-speed manual transmission and a 6-speed torque convertor automatic transmission. The Thar's power output ranges from 118 PS to 152 PS, while its torque output ranges from 300 Nm to 320 Nm.

In Water

The Thar's dimensions are impressive, with a length of 3985 mm, a width of 1820 mm, and a height of 1855 mm. The SUV also features a ground clearance of 226 mm, making it well-suited for off-road adventures.

One of the Thar's standout features is its 4x4 system, which allows the SUV to tackle tough terrains with ease. The system features a manual low-ratio 4x4 transfer case, which enables the SUV to switch between two-wheel drive and four-wheel drive modes. The Thar also features a range of off-road-oriented features, including removable doors, washable interiors, roof-mounted speakers, and a washable floor.

On Road

In terms of safety, the Thar has received 4/5 stars for both adult and child occupant protection in Global NCAP's crash tests. The Thar's interior is modern and feature-packed, with a touch infotainment system, steering controls, connected car technology, keyless entry, and more. The SUV's exterior design is rugged and aggressive, with high wheel arches, prominent bumpers, and a rear-placed stepney.

Overall, the Mahindra Thar is a capable and popular off-road SUV that offers a range of engines, transmissions, and features to suit different needs and preferences. Its impressive off-road capability, modern interior, and rugged exterior design make it a great choice for those who want a vehicle that can handle tough terrains and look good doing it.
